Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy is taken seriously. This policy explains what information is collected when you submit an application through this website, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit the application form, we collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Age
  • City of residence
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your stated goal

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the purpose of evaluating your application and establishing contact with you. Specifically:

  • To assess whether the program is a suitable fit
  • To reach out to you directly via email or phone
  • To personalize your coaching program if accepted

Your data will never be used for unsolicited marketing or sold to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Processing

Form submissions are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. By submitting the form, you acknowledge that your data passes through Formspree's infrastructure. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to their handling of your data.

Submitted data is stored securely and accessible only to ANDREA Coaching. It is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected.

4. Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties for their commercial purposes. The only third-party service involved in data handling is Formspree, used exclusively to deliver form submissions.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly via the application form on the main page.

6. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics software. No data is collected passively from your visit.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Governing Law

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of Italy and complies with applicable European data protection regulations (GDPR).

9. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please reach out through the application form on the main page and clearly state your request.
